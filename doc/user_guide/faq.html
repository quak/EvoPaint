Why my pixels don´t move?
Your pixels are all right but their behavior rule don´t allow them to perform a kind of an action.


QA
What is a rule?
A rule defines the behavior of a pixel. Most of the time this behavior depends on interactions between pixels. So its possible that a pixel can move, assimilate others, it can copy itself, it can change its energy or its color and it can proceed with other pixels.


QA
How does the color effects the evolution?
The color of a pixel is a comparison possibility when we create a rule. It is possible for a pixel to change its color when he for example proceeds with a partner.

What is fairy dust?
Fairy dust is a paint option. Sometimes its nice to paint into the evolution with a random colored brush.

How can i create rules?
